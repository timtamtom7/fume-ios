## Full Description

FumeMac is your second brain — entirely local, entirely private.

Dump notes, articles, voice memos, and images into one place. Then ask FumeMac anything about what you've saved. It searches your knowledge base and answers from your own notes, entirely on your Mac.

**How it works**
Add content in seconds — type a note, paste an article URL, record a thought, photograph a document. FumeMac stores everything on your device and builds a searchable knowledge base from your own information.

Ask questions in plain language. FumeMac semantically searches across all your sources and returns answers with citations back to the original content.

**Privacy first**
Everything runs on-device using Apple's Neural Engine and NaturalLanguage framework. Your data never leaves your Mac. No cloud. No subscriptions. No data collection. No internet required for AI features.

## Privacy Policy Summary

FumeMac processes all data locally on your device using on-device machine learning frameworks (NaturalLanguage, Speech, Vision). No data is transmitted to external servers or third parties. No user accounts required. No analytics or tracking. All source content and embeddings remain entirely on your Mac.

**Frameworks used (all on-device):**
- NaturalLanguage — semantic embeddings and search
- Speech — voice memo transcription
- Vision — image analysis
- AVFoundation — audio recording
- CoreML — local model inference (where applicable)
